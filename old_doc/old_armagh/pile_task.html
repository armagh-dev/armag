The Pile Task's Role

The pile task plays a particularly purposeful part in the process. It keeps track of the progress of a set of documents as it goes through the scriptorium.

Documents are grouped together in piles to help maintain documents in time order. It is assumed that a pile represents all the documents for the time period covered by the pile. If piles are delivered to librarians in time order, and the documents within each pile are delivered in time order, the customer will get all documents in time order.
