The Scribe's Role

The job of the scribe is to translate works.

The Scribe's Behaviors

  • A scribe monitors for unclaimed document tasks and claims one.
  • After claiming a document task, the scribe determines what transcription is required by the task, finds the source document in the active works, performs the transcription, writes the new document back to the works, and updates the document task.
  • If the scribe encounters errors during transcription, it updates the document task with error details.
