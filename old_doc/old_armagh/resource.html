The Resource's Role

The resource information provides the technical details and current status of the servers and services supporting the scriptorium.

The Resource's Attributes

Dependent on the detailed architecture. Should include, in general:

  • servers - hostname, ping status, cpu utilization, disk utilitization, i/o utilization
  • services - name, status, relevant parameters
