The Provisioner's Role

The provisioner collects piles of documents from sources and tees them up for transcription.

The Provisioner's Behaviors

  • The provisioner monitors the task pool to find the next unclaimed pile task and claims it.
  • After the provisioner successfully claims a pile task, it collects a pile of documents from a source according to its agreement, and stores them with works.
  • After the provisioner successfully collects and stores a pile of documents, it creates and posts document tasks for scribes to transcribe the documents, and updates the pile task to alert librarians that a new pile is being worked.
  • If the provisioner encounters errors while attempting to contact a source to collect a pile, it updates the pile task with the errors.
  • If the provisioner encounters errors while attempting to pull specific documents in a pile, it includes the errors in the document task.
