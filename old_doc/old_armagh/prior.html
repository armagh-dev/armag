The Prior's Role

The only job of the prior, or "vice abbot", is to be sure the abbot is doing his job.

The Prior's Behaviors

  • The prior periodically verifies the health of the abbot and, if he is unresponsive, restarts him.
  • The prior notifies the administrator if he is unable to carry out this function.
  • The prior shuts down if it detects another prior with a higher process ID.
